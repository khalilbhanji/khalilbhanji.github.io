The heroes that I most admire embody the following characteristics:

  • Public Image - Anyone who sees them, respects the hard work they put in to make sure they look good in the public eye. This is much harder than it may seem.
  • Down to Earth - No matter if it is someone in their inner circle or a stanger/fan, they understand that everyone has struggles and that kindness is always the best attitude.
  • Dedication - Whether in the gym, the office, or the studio, they are always putting in extra work to build their character and build up those around them.
  • The Numbers - These folks don't play around. They make sure that their money and views are always flowing to them no matter what.
  • Powerhouse - They are simply a cut above the rest and hold themselves to a higher standard then we ever could.

What makes a hero? A hero is someone that we look up to. Heroes embody what we truly value most. Therefore who we select as role models says a lot about who we are as people.

Dwayne Johnson is one of the most versatile celebrities on the planet. I love his work ethic, being not only an athlete, but a movie star, and even a singer. He is always nice and presents himself well. He shows me who I would like to become when I get older.
